## Summary

FitDiary is built to fully respect your privacy. **The app works completely
offline, collects no personal data, sends nothing to any server, and shares
nothing with third parties.** Everything you enter stays solely on your device.

\---

## 1\. Data we do NOT collect

FitDiary **does not collect, transmit, or share** any personal or identifying
data. Specifically, we do not collect:

* personal or contact details (name, email, phone);
* device or advertising identifiers;
* location data;
* analytics, usage statistics, or crash reports;
* biometric data.

The app uses no accounts, requires no online sign-up, and performs no login.

## 2\. Data you enter (stored locally only)

The information you type into the app — for example your profile (name, sex, age,
height, weight, goals), meals and calories, workouts, menus, progress photos,
menstrual-cycle data, hydration, and settings — is stored **only in your
device's internal storage**.

This data:

* never leaves your device;
* is not accessible to the developer or to any third party;
* is never sent over the internet (the app uses no network connection).

## 3\. Camera and images

The app may open the system camera or gallery so you can add progress photos.
Selected images are stored locally only and are **never uploaded anywhere**. The
app does not access your gallery in the background.

## 4\. Notifications

FitDiary may show local reminders (such as the "time to drink water" reminder).
These notifications are generated entirely on the device and involve no data
transmission. On Android 13 and later you are asked for notification permission;
you may deny or revoke it at any time in your system settings.

## 5\. Permissions requested

|Permission|Purpose|
|-|-|
|Notifications (`POST\_NOTIFICATIONS`)|Show the hydration reminder.|
|Vibrate (`VIBRATE`)|Optional vibration for the notification.|
|Camera (system)|Take progress photos (launched at your request).|

The app does **not** request the Internet permission.

## 6\. Third-party services

FitDiary **does not embed** any analytics, advertising, social-network, or other
third-party SDKs that collect data.

## 7\. Children's data

The app is not specifically directed at children and, in any case, collects no
data from any user.

## 8\. Data retention and deletion

Because your data lives only on your device, you remain in full control. You can
delete it at any time:

* by clearing the app data in Android settings
(Settings → Apps → FitDiary → Storage → Clear data);
* or by uninstalling the app.

Uninstalling permanently removes all associated data.

## 9\. Security

Data stays confined to the app's private storage area on the device, protected by
the Android operating system. Because there is no network transmission, there is
no risk of data being intercepted in transit.

Any updates to this policy will be posted on this same page, with the
"Last updated" date at the top revised accordingly.
